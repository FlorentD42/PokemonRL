What is it?

An attempt at making a game in JavaScript. It is meant to be a bit Pokémon, and a bit rogue-like.

Each run will follow this pattern:

  1. The Player starts in a city and chooses a starter Pokémon(tbd)
  2. They then have to travel a randomly generated world, containing four cities (not counting the starting one) and a league, with roads between these maps
  3. The Player must first collect four badges from each city, battling trainers and catching wild Pokémons in between
  4. The Player battles against the league and his team becomes the Champion's for the next run.
  5. Restart until Pokédex is full

Some more details...

The Pokémons stats are similar from Pokémon Go (CPs and hidden values). Similarly, they have only two attacks, a basic and a charged one. They earn XP after each victory, which increases their CPs. Evolutions depends on the CPs. There is no K.O., only Death.

The fights are meant to be fast. Turn based, 3v3. The basic attack generates energy which can be used for a more powerful charged attack.

Each map between cities has at least one random event, including: wild Pokémon appears, trainers battles, evolution stones merchant, Pokémon trader, items (e.g. Berries)

Cities will have a Gym, a Pokémon Center to heal HPs and a Shop to buy Items.

Catching a wild Pokémon will require Pokéballs and Berries or in some cases, a Battle.
